Kingdom Hearts I

Kingdom hearts is a single player linear storyline set in a crossover universe of Final Fantasy and Disney! The main character Sora becomes the Keyblade wielder
and is destined to defeat the heartless and save all the worlds.(in the game of course) His world is devoured by darkness and while in the Hub world he meets his
new companions Donald Duck and goofy where they are designated by King Mickey to stay by his side and assist him in stopping the darkness. He evenetually
achieves his goal and defeats the main Heartless antagonist Ansem.

Kingdom Hearts II

Kingdom hearts 2 takes place after Chain of memories, which is basis for the Backgroud art you see for this website. Sora had put to sleep and had his memories taken.
The game however starts off with his nobody and for 2 hours you go through a sort of tutorial mode. Roxas is eventually finds sora and reunites with him and this is where
you play as the main antagonist again. Sora reawakens to a world being tormented by darkness again and restarts his original journey of stopping it again. This time around
Orginization XII is causing havoc and you have to stop them. Sora goes through a whole plethora of Disney worlds again fighting heartless and nobodies along with various
Orginization XII members. At the end of the game you visit the World that never was and fight every remaining Orginization XII member and then Battle Xemnas the leader.
The game ends with you and all your friends at the beach of your home world Desinty Islands. You reciever a letter at the end bring you to Kingdom Hearts III.

Kingdom Hearts III

The game starts off with you needing to learn the power of awakening. This prompts you to travel to various Disney worlds fighting heartless trying to learn the power of awakening.
After a while you learn you always had the power of awakening. But you still have to stop the main antagonist Xeanort and his is assembling the dark XIII to fight the light XII in order
to recreate the X-Blade(Chi blade) so that he can unlocl Kingdom hearts which is a heart shaped moon in everygame that releases hearts. Sora dies in the end world along with everyone else
when they team is overwhelemd by heartless. He then is brought back to life and then fights Xeanort where he wins and the Xeanort Saga ends.

Our Texts that we have gathered are the game scripts of each of the main titles of Kingdom Hearts. 1, 2, and 3. The first game was released in 2002
and the second installment was released in 2005. After this fans had to wait 14 years to the release of the 3 main installment of the Kingdom hearts franchise in 2019.
We chose the main titles are the third game was released so far apart from the first two. This could lead differences in story telling. Causing some characters to become
more or less important because of how long inbetween games it was.

We ended up analyzing nouns used as a comparrison between KH1 and the 3 main titles together in order to analyze differences between the first titles and the series as a whole. We also made svg graphs
